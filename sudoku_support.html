    <h2>Common Questions</h2>

    <h2>🎯 Goal of the Game</h2>

    <p>

      Fill in the empty cells so that each row, each column, and each 3×3 area contains the digits 1 through 9 exactly once.

    </p>


    <h2>⚡ Difficulty Levels</h2>

    <p>

      All boards are generated and tested for solvability based on difficulty, number of clues, and required solving techniques before play begins.

    </p>

    <ul>

      <li><strong>Easy</strong> – many pre-filled numbers, several logical moves available from the start. Perfect for beginners.</li>

      <li><strong>Medium</strong> – fewer clues, requires combining basic techniques.</li>

      <li><strong>Hard</strong> – minimal clues, advanced solving methods needed (Locked Candidates, Naked Pairs, X-Wing).</li>

      <li><strong>Expert</strong> – very few starting numbers, often requires advanced strategies like Swordfish or XY-Wing.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>🤝 Challenge a Friend</h2>

    <p>

      When you solve a board, tap <em>Challenge a Friend</em> to generate a code.  

      The code can be sent via message or email, and your friend can paste it into the app to play the exact same Sudoku with identical difficulty.  

      The code also includes your result: time, errors, and hints used.

    </p>


    <h2>📥 Accept a Challenge</h2>

    <p>

      To play a challenge you’ve received, copy the message and paste it into <em>Accept Challenge</em> inside the app.

    </p>


    <h2>💡 Tips</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>Use <strong>Hint</strong> if you get stuck – it fills in one correct number.</li>

      <li><strong>Undo/Redo</strong> lets you backtrack or repeat moves.</li>

      <li>Pre-filled numbers are always correct and cannot be changed.</li>

      <li>Red numbers are wrong, green borders highlight the same digit across the board.</li>

    </ul>
